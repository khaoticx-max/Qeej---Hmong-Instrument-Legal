🎵 Qeej – Hmong Instrument

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Short version: This app collects absolutely no personal data. Everything you do — your recordings, settings, and preferences — stays entirely on your device and is never sent anywhere.

1. Who We Are

Qeej – Hmong Instrument is an interactive virtual instrument app that lets you play, record, and customize the sound of the Qeej, a traditional Hmong wind instrument. This app is developed and maintained by Kao Xiong.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data — none, zero. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location or IP address
  • Your device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Your recordings or audio files
  • Your usage patterns, analytics, or crash reports
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are used in this app.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All app data — including your custom recordings, sound preferences, and settings — is stored locally on your device only using standard browser storage (IndexedDB and localStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Is removed when you uninstall the app
  • Is fully under your control at all times

4. Microphone Access

This app requests access to your device's microphone only when you choose to use the "Record Your Own Sound" feature to create a custom instrument. Microphone access is:

  • Only activated when you explicitly initiate a recording
  • Used solely to capture audio for your custom instrument sounds
  • Stored locally on your device only — never transmitted or uploaded
  • Never used for surveillance, background listening, or any other purpose

You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings. The app will continue to function normally; only the custom recording feature will be unavailable.

5. Children and All Ages

Qeej – Hmong Instrument is suitable for users of all ages. Because we collect no data from anyone, there are no special data risks for younger users. No personal information is gathered from any user regardless of age.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties — because we do not collect any data to begin with.

7. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs that would collect data about you.

8. Security

Since no data is transmitted from your device, there is no server-side data at risk of a breach. Your recordings and settings are as secure as your own device.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify users through an in-app notice or an app update. The updated policy will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after notification constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control. You can delete your recordings and reset your settings directly within the app, or remove all data by uninstalling the app.
